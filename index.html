How it works

Three phases. One team. Real handoff.

01

We audit what you have

One week of discovery. We map your workflows, identify where time is being wasted, and find the three to five things that would have the highest ROI if automated. You get a plain-language report. No PowerPoint.

02

We build it together

5–8 weeks of actual building. We'd rather show up on week three with a working voice agent prototype than week one with a deck. You see it working before it ships. You test it before it goes live.

03

We hand it off completely

Documentation. A Loom walkthrough for every system. 30-day support window. Your team should be able to run everything we built without us — and if they can't, that's on us to fix. Optional retainer for what comes next.
