About

We automate what slows you down.

Most businesses waste hours on work that a well-built system could handle in seconds. Evofusion builds those systems so your team can focus on what actually matters.

Mission
"To make the boring parts of running a business disappear, so the people running it can do work that actually matters."

Every business has a core job to do. Everything around that core job — the scheduling, the follow-ups, the routing, the reporting — is overhead. We make the overhead disappear.

Vision
"A world where every small business has the same operational leverage as a tech company — without having to become one."

Right now, the tools that power big tech — voice AI, workflow automation, AI-trained support — are only accessible to companies that can hire a team to build and maintain them. We're changing that.

What we believe

Four things we'll never compromise on.

Quiet by default

Good software fades into the background. We don't build automations that need a dashboard with seventeen tabs, or chatbots that try to befriend you, or voice agents that pretend to be human. If you notice it, we did it wrong.

Plain English

We don't say "agentic hyperautomation orchestration platform." We say "this thing reads your emails and files them." If we can't explain it to your bookkeeper, we built it wrong. Plain language is a form of respect.

One team, real handoff

We don't disappear after launch. Every project comes with documentation, a Loom walkthrough for every system, and a 30-day support window. Your team should be able to run everything we built without us — and if they can't, that's on us to fix.

Craft over speed-to-pitch

We'd rather show up on week three with a working voice agent prototype than week one with a deck. The work is the proof. We don't sell proposals — we build things and show you they work.

Fit

We're honest about who we're for.

Good fit
Founders and ops leads at SMBs
Who feel the pressure to "do something with AI" but don't know where to start — or where to find someone trustworthy.
Service businesses where the phone is the front door
Clinics, dealerships, home services, real estate, agencies — where an overworked front-of-house team is the bottleneck.
Professional service firms drowning in repeatable work
Legal, accounting, healthcare admin — teams that are doing the same things over and over and know it.
E-commerce and DTC brands
Whose support inbox never sleeps and whose website is doing 70% of what it could.
Companies that have outgrown Squarespace + a VA
But aren't ready for Salesforce and a Big 4 consultant. We're the studio in between.
Not a fit
Enterprise companies
We're a studio, not a consultancy. If you need 50 people and 18 months, we'll point you toward someone who is built for that.
Pre-revenue startups
You don't have enough process to automate yet. Come back when you have a repeatable business — then there's something to work with.
Anyone shopping on price
We're not trying to win on price. If you're looking for the cheapest option, we're not it — and we're honest about that.
How we're different

We sit in a gap nobody else is filling well.

vs. AI freelancers
Fiverr, Upwork

Freelancers deliver one thing and disappear. You get a brittle Zap, a chatbot that hallucinates your pricing, and no one to call when it breaks. We deliver production-grade systems with documentation and a real handoff — and we're accountable for 30 days after launch.

vs. web agencies
Traditional design studios

A traditional web agency ships a beautiful site and considers the job done. We don't. Voice agents, chatbots, and automation are in the DNA of everything we build — not bolted on after the fact. A form submission from our websites isn't just an email. It's a lead already qualified and routed.

vs. enterprise consultancies
Accenture, BCG X

We ship in weeks, not quarters. We don't bill $400/hr for slide decks. We're a studio — we build, we test, we hand off. If you need a 200-page strategy document and 18 months of consulting retainer, we'll point you elsewhere. If you need working software in 6–10 weeks, that's us.
